Timeline

21st February 2020
Friday

HAVELOCK ISLAND

Arrival at Port Blair in the morning by flight. Depart in a ferry to Havelock Island. Depart at 12 pm and arrive at 2 pm.
Ferry charges: Rs 1100 per person.
Upon reaching Havelock, check-in at beachside resort. Day start after lunch. Later head to visit one of Asia's finest beaches - Radhanagar Beach. Explore this white sand beach and bask in the beauty of this natural wonder. Later back to the resort where you can relax and unwind. 

22nd February 2020
Saturday

HAVELOCK AND ADVENTURE 

After early breakfast (depart at 9 am), enjoy a unique excursion to the Famous Elephanta Beach - popular for its adventure activities. The water is Turquoise Blue and crystal clear makes it an ideal destination for Snorkeling and Sea walk. There are two ways to get to the Elephant Beach from Havelock Island. First, you can take a ferry (extra charges) from Havelock Dock, and you will reach the beach in around 20 minutes. Secondly, you can take a walk through a serene, beautiful path, which takes around 15-20 minutes to reach the beach. Upon reaching the beach, you can snorkel and enjoy the view of Live Corals. One can enjoy the Sea walk - an underwater activity with a huge mask through which oxygen is supplied, enabling a walk on the sea bed in the beautiful underwater world.

23rd February 2020
Sunday

NEIL ISLAND

After breakfast, board a ferry cruise to Neil Island.
Depart at 8:30 am and arrive at 9:30 am.
Ferry charges: Rs 900 per person.
Upon reaching the island check-in to the resort. Take some rest and then proceed to Bharatpur Beach - later visit the famous Natural rock formation o the Sitapur Beach which is also known as the Howrah Bridge.  

24th February 2020
Monday

Depart at 9:45 am and arrive at 12:45 pm.

FAQ

What about transportation in Andaman?

Cabs and rentals are available in Port Blair and Havelock Island. Renting two wheelers is good options. Day rates for two wheelers : Rs. 500. For any form of transport, it is advised to prebook.

What is double sharing & triple sharing?

Room sharing between two people is double and three people is triple.

What are the tour dates and why?

The tour dates are from 21st Feb 2019 to 25th Feb 2019. The only public holiday in feb is on 21st (Maha Shivratri), since it is a long weekend it will not be an issue for people who have to take leave from work.
